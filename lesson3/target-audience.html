Target Audience

The target audience for this website is working adults ages 18 to 50, with an income of 30 to 100K. This audience will be highly educated and expect to have accurate weather information so they can go on vacation or enjoy a weekend out in nature with their family. The adults in this age group are more active and enjoy being outdoors. They need to know the weather conditions for safety concerns, for recreation, and for comfort.

The website visitors expect the weather forecast to be accurate and applicable to their location. They will use the website to plan their parties, barbeques, ski trips, soccer games, weddings, picnics, swimming, hiking, football games, etc.

The target audience will visit this website from home, work, or while sitting at a ball game. They will access forecastforfun.com from their mobile phones, tablets, laptops, or desktops. Since most people visiting this site will be active and on the go, they will most frequently use cell phones to access this weather website.

The Forecast For Fun website will help its visitors answer the following questions:

  • Will it rain today?
  • Should I bring a coat?
  • Will the water in the pool be warm?
  • Is it too windy for a barbeque?
  • Will it be safe to hike in the canyon?
  • Should I put on sunscreen?
  • Will the ball game get rained out?
  • Is it going to snow?
  • How are the weather conditions on the highway?
  • Should we postpone the party?
  • Will I be able to get a suntan today?

Personas

Here are examples of our target audience and some of their demographics. The following personas are a representation of those who will likely visit this weather website.

Persona #1
Name: Andrew Caldwell
Job Title: Electrical Engineer
Demographics: 38 years old, married, father of 2, has a BSEE

He is intelligent, organized, and very efficient. He is a manager of a team of research and development engineers. He is proficient in the use of application software and computer systems. He uses the internet daily and expects quality and reliability from the websites he frequents. It is important for him to be prepared for any event he plans and therefore needs a current weather forecast from forecastforfun.com.

Persona #2
Name: Elizabeth Jameson
Job Title: Dental Hygenist
Demographics: 29 years old, married, mother of one, AA degree, licensed Dental Hygenist

She is witty, patient, and professional. She works with dentists to provide quality dental care to their patients. She is thorough in her work and is pleasant to be around. She is active on social media and stays current on local and national news. She works fulltime and cherishes her weekends. She will visit forecastforfun.com so that she can be prepared for the weather conditions when she attends sporting events or goes camping with her family.
