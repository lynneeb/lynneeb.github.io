Site Purpose

The purpose of this Forecast For Fun website is to provide daily and weekly temperature, humidity, and wind forecasts. It will give accurate weather conditions by location.

The goal of this site: Provide weather information to help people be prepared as they venture outdoors.

The website visitors will be able to make judgment calls about the weather. The weather forecast will help them know whether to grab a coat, an umbrella, put on some snow boots, or just stay home.

Here are the main objectives for this weather website:

  • Provide daily and weekly weather forecasts
  • Offer weather safety alerts
  • Create a weather website that is popular among active adults
  • Draw customers who love the outdoors
  • Receive positive feedback from those who use our site
  • Gain new customers due to favorable referrals

This website will attract people who love traveling, attending ball games, hunting, fishing, camping, hiking, and those who are planning special events. The information provided within this website will allow customers to be prepared for the current or upcoming weather conditions. It will help by giving them the information they need to protect themselves and their family from severe thunderstorms, violent tornadoes, devastating earthquakes, freezing blizzards, and life-threatening flash-floods. These weather forecasts are an essential service provided to the public.
