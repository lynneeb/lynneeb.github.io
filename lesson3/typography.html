Typography

The main reason people come to a website is to read the words on the page, so the way text is presented is very important. Within a website, 90% of the design is attributed to typography. It makes the words readable and attractive, providing a positive user experience.

The typeface will also convey intent and emotion to those who visit the site. In order to have a clean and ordered design, it is recommended to use only 2 to 4 fonts. To achieve a professional look, the typography must remain consistent throughout the website.

In the digital world, typography is the extension of a brand. Typography portrays the image of the business and is essential for the branding of its products. It is crucial to choose a font that will not only enhance image and branding, but also be appropriate within the industry. Before choosing the typographic style for a website, carefully consider the message the business desires to convey.

To enhance the flow of content within the website, the text must be properly aligned. Alignment adds structure to the design and helps to create a good experience for those who visit the site. An appropriate font size will also reflect a professional image of the business. It is important for the text to be large enough to be legible.
