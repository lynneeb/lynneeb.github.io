PARC: Repetition

Apple

This Apple image demonstrates the principle of repetition. The bubbly style and bright colors are duplicated on the iPad as well as on the advertisement behind it. It is effective in catching your eye and holding your attention.

PARC: Contrast

Whatsupnewp

The contrast in this Tiffany & Co. advertisement is very alluring. The picture of the classy woman in the black dress next to the bright blue background is very effective in drawing your attention to the gold rings.

PARC: Proximity

Visme

This IHOP flyer is a great example of proximity. Your eyes move to the top of the page where the large, bold, heading, "Kids Eat Free" captures your interest. Relative information is grouped just underneath the heading. Likewise, throughout the page, content with similar information are placed within the same proximity. This design showcases an organized and easy-to-read page.
