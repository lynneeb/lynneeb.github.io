Preston, Idaho

by Lynnette Beatty

The city of Preston is located in Franklin County in the state of Idaho. Preston has a total area of 6.66 square miles. Close to the city is a popular recreation area called Cub River Canyon. It’s climate is warm and humid in the summertime at around 100 deg. F. The temperature can get below 0 deg. F in the winter. According to the 2010 census, there were 5,204 people in Preston.

In late July, the Franklin County parade and ‘That Famous Preston Night Rodeo’ are held. The parade festivities include floats and advertisements for local businesses. The rodeo is held at night and features barrel racing and bull riding. These two events remain a popular tradition for the city.

A large portion of the movie, “Napoleon Dynamite”, was filmed in Preston. The movie’s creators, Jared and Jerusha Hess live in Preston.
