Target Audience

The target audience for this website is adults ages 18 to 80, with an income of 20 to 100K. This audience may be retired, stay-at-home moms, or working adults with children. The adults in this age group are faithful member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. They need accommodations for themselves and their families while doing temple work.

The website visitors expect to find lodging that is safe and comfortable. They need a hotel that is in close proximity to a temple. These people are looking for convenience and a nice place to gather with loved ones.

The target audience will visit this website from home or work. They will access templeinnandsuites.com from their mobile phones, tablets, laptops, or desktops. Since the people visiting this site travel often, they will most frequently use cell phones to access this website.

The Temple Inn & Suites website will help its visitors answer the following questions:

  • Will the rooms be large enough for my whole family?
  • Will there be enough beds?
  • Will the beds be clean and comfortable?
  • Is breakfast provided?
  • How close is the hotel to the temple?
  • Is there a swimming pool?
  • Will there be adequate parking?
  • Is there a shuttle to the temple?
  • Do the rooms have a T.V?
  • Is free wireless internet provided?
  • Is there a kitchenette?
  • What other amenities are available?

Personas

These are examples of our target audience and some of their demographics. The following personas are a representation of our website visitors.

Persona #1
Name: Keith Lambdin
Job Title: Retired Teacher
Demographics: 61 years old, married, father of 3, has a BS in Secondary Education.

He is a temple worker in the baptistry. He is friendly, has a good sense of humor and is intelligent. He is a great storyteller and is a patient teacher and mentor. He loves to read and explore nature. He only uses the internet when absolutely necessary, because it intimidates him. It is important for him to have a safe and comfortable place for him and his wife to stay, so he will visit templeinnandsuites.com to make a reservation.

Persona #2
Name: Amanda Davis
Job Title: Homemaker
Demographics: 32 years old, married, mother of 4, AA degree in Childhood Development.

She is happy, compassionate, and loves doing temple work for her deceased ancestors. She enjoys making crafts with her children and teaching them about nature and the Gospel of Jesus Christ. She is very organized and keeps a clean home. She is faithful in her calling as Young Women's president and sets a good example for the youth in her ward. She will visit templeinnandsuites.com to book a reservation for the young women in her ward, so they will have a comfortable place to stay while doing temple baptisms.
