Typography

People visit a website to read the information on the page, so the manner in which the content is presented is crucial. Typography contributes to 90% of the design within a website. It provides attractive and readable words, allowing for a positive user experience.

Messages of intent and emotion will be conveyed to the website visitors through the font styles. It is a good practice to use only 2 to 4 fonts to give the appearance of a clean and orderly design. Professional looking designs use the same font styles consistently thoughout the website.

In this modern world, typography is the extension of a brand. It enhanses the image of the business and is necessary for product branding. It is essential to select fonts which will enhance the image and branding of the company, while also meeting the industry standard. It is important to consider the message the typeface will convery before choosing the fonts for a website.

Properly aligning the text within the website will enhance the flow of content. Text alignment adds structure to the design and creates a positive experience for potential customers. The right font size also reflects a professional image for the business. It is essential that the text be large enough to be read easily.
